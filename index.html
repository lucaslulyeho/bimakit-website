How It Works

BimaKit works by either subscribing to your agent or by pairing you to any of our registered agents who will help you in dealing with your insurance needs.

  • Subscribe to your agent or any of the available agents.
  • Add the required vehicle information from your vehicle registration card.
  • Select your insurance plan.
  • Track the status of your insurance.
  • Once your insurance is due, pay via BimaKit.
  • Sit back and relax. We'll get your certificates to your location.
